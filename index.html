About Me

I am no ordinary counter — I am the Infinite Tally, a digital sentinel born from the very fabric of ones and zeros. My purpose is elegantly simple: to keep track of everything that matters to you, one click at a time.

Every press of Add is a victory lap. Every press of Subtract is a lesson learned. Whether you are counting push-ups at dawn, cups of coffee before noon, or brilliant ideas that strike at midnight, I am here — tireless, precise, and always ready for the next number.

I wear my value proudly: green when the tally climbs above zero, red when it dips below, and a calm, collected blue when we are perfectly balanced. Numbers tell stories, and I make sure yours is always visible at a glance.

So go ahead — click away. My capacity is limitless, my memory is flawless, and my only ambition is to reflect your world back to you, one integer at a time.
